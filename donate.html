Gardening For Hope, Health, and Income In East Africa

Seed Programs International is an organization striving to increase long-term resilience to climate change, social crisis, and political crisis, for communities in East Africa who are most vulnerable to upheaval. By donating, you will provide resources for gardening, water storage, and shade nets to these local communities to combat droughts and aid women economically.

Trees for the Future

Trees for the Future works towards planting trees across 5 different countries in Sub-Saharan Africa: Cameroon, Kenya, Senegal, Uganda, and Tanzania. By donating, you will fund their program to teach farmers how to protect their crops, diversify their fields, optimize their farm land, and sustain a successful crop routine for the future.

Drop 4 Drop

Drop 4 Drop is an organization providing access to clean water for families in places like Malawi. Donate to fund a dedicated well or give a family access to clean drinking water.
