We are sowing okra

Zai Pits in Niger

Shared by Amy on 6/19/2020 at 3:28pm WAT

The women in our community are hard at work sowing okra in our zai pits. We put the seeds in a open area that reaches sun, since okras need around 8 hours of sunlight a day. We are using a technique to put manure in our zai pits to collect water and feed our plants. So far, this has been working great.
